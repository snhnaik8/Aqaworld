About

Aqua World

Established in the early 21st Century, Aqua World is a brand synonymous with aqua tourism in Goa. In 2007, Our first river cruise, Coral Queen set sail in the serene waters of Mandovi. Ever since, it has become a favourite amongst travellers and settlers alike who wish to explore glimpses of tradional music, dance, food and beverage against the backdrop of plush green hills,  calm, blue water and gorgeous amber sunsets.

With the demands increasing by the year, we added yet another cruise, Coral Queen II in 2016.

Whatever be the occasion, our endeavour will always be to deliver soulful luxury and leave you with a vessel full of memories.  Welcome aboard!
